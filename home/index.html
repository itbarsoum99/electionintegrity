The Election Integrity Project works to increase knowledge of the US' democratic processes, using free and unbiased curricula, workshops, and handbooks. Our goals are to increase the level of knowledge about the United States' democracy among students, and to encourage eligible voters to register and vote.

Featured Project

Election Transparency Index
Based on transparency of statistics, mail-in voting, ease of voter registration/election, and photo ID laws, the index takes the average of scores from all four of these categories and converts it into a letter grade for every state.

Featured State

  • North Carolina's election practices receive an 81 on the Election Transparency Index.
  • North Carolina is a crucial swing state. Get information on voting in North Carolina with our Voter Guide.

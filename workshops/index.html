Workshops

The Election Integrity Project will have 5 workshops for one hour weekly, surrounding Election Day 2020. Ages 10-12 will meet on Mondays from 5-6 Eastern, and ages 13-15 will meet on Wednesdays from 4-5 PM Eastern.


Requirements for participation:

  • One (1) referral from a teacher with a connection to the student commending the student, as well as that teacher's name, school, and contact information
  • Open-mindedness and willingness to discuss politics
  • $30 to our GoFundMe Charity or timestamped image of student with person in family wearing 'I Voted' sticker.
  • Do NOT pay the donation until you are contacted; if there are not three people registered for each workshop, it will be dissolved.

Workshops will begin on Wednesday, October 14 (first Monday will be Monday, October 19) and will continue through Wednesday, November 11 and Monday, November 16. There will be an additional session on Tuesday, November 3 (Election Day) for all members to discuss the implications of that day. For more information, please contact me at itbarsoum99@gmail.com.

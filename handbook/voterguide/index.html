The Election Handbook

The Election Handbook provides a nonpartisan voter guide for states that receive a grade of C+ or worse on the Election Transparency Index. In these states, it is considered more difficult than average to vote. The voter guide also gives guidance for the key swing states of North Carolina, Ohio, Florida, Pennsylvania, Arizona, Michigan, and Wisconsin.

Alabama

    Grade on the ETI:
  • 79/C+
    • To register:
  • Eligible adults must register by October 20.
    • To vote by mail:
  • No excuse needed.
  • You must request an absentee ballot to vote.
  • A photo ID must be submitted with the application to vote by mail.
    • To vote in person:
  • A photo ID or an identity affidavit is required to vote.

Arizona

Swing State

    Grade on the ETI:
  • 85/B
    • To register:
  • Eligible adults must register by October 5.

    • To vote in person:
  • A non-photo ID or identity affidavit is required to vote.
